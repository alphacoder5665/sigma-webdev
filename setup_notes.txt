

1. Right click and select "command palette" OR Ctrl+Shift+P.
    :- to open command palette
        :- search "screencast mode" select it.

2. open  settings.
    :- search "screencast"
        :- set "Vertical Offset" to "1". 

3. Mobile Preview Tip

    Open CMD 
        :- Type "ipconfig"
            :- Check "IPv4" and copy it then..

    ## Must ensure that your connected wifi is on "Private Network" not on "Public Network"

    Open settings:
        : - search "live preview"
            :- change "Host IP" from "127.0.0.1" to "192.168.1.2"
